Instructions


Round 1



Read all instructions carefully.

1) Answer the sections in "lowercase" BRITISH SPELLED word/ single-spaced words ONLY in case of short answer type. 

2) Any abbreviations must be written in full form.

3) For example, if the answer is color, answer it as: colour

4) In case, if the answer needs multiple words, for example, Michael Scott, answer it as: michael scott.

5) If your answer is in numeral values, for example, 78, answer it as: 78

6) If the answer is LOL enter: laugh out loud

7) Special characters are allowed.

8) MCQ Questions will have multiple options out of which one of them is correct.

9) There are 10 each of short, long and common tasks. 

10) ST stands for Short task and this abbreviation is used to give a serial number to each task.

11) Each short task is worth 2 points but has a negative 1 on incorrect answers

12) Each long task is worth 3 points with no negative marking.

13) Each common task is worth 4 points with no negative marking.

14) In case of a tie, the participant with least amount of time taken ranks higher.

15) Stay calm. Try to finish all tasks and save your crew.





AIB - Acumen @ TechTatva 2020, MIT Manipal.
